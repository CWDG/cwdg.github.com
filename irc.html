IRC Chat

Why Chat?

We'll try to hang out in IRC on a regular basis to help people in a live chat format. We are cultivating a community where we can help each other throughout the week and share their experiences.

Better Options?

There are better programs to use for IRC. These will automatically connect and stay open longer. I've heard good things about: XChat, Pidgin, Adium, KVIrc, Colloquy, and Limechat. You can find a much larger, more exhaustive list on Wikipedia and their extensive comparison.
